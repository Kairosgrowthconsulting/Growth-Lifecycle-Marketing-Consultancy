What's included

Funnel Audit

Full analysis of your conversion funnel using GA4, Hotjar, and qualitative research.

A/B Testing Programme

Hypothesis-driven experiments on pages, flows, and CTAs — with statistical rigour.

UX Recommendations

Specific, prioritised UX improvements backed by data and conversion principles.

Checkout Optimisation

Reducing cart abandonment through trust signals, friction removal, and payment UX.

Typical outcome: 15–35% conversion rate improvement within 90 days. Average cart abandonment recovery of 22% through systematic optimisation.
